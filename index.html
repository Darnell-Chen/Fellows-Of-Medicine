Fellows of Medicine

Our main goal at Fellows of Medicine is to is to empower high school students with equitable access to resources, mentorship, and industry networking opportunities in medicine. We cultivate a supportive community where experienced students mentor aspiring individuals, facilitating their seamless transition into healthcare careers.

About Us

As a pioneering organization, Fellows of Medicine is dedicated to fostering equal opportunities for high school students passionate about medicine. Our platform connects these aspiring individuals with industry professionals, offering invaluable networking and mentorship opportunities

Media Intern Program

Our Media Intern Program empowers high school students to create and share engaging, informative posts on medical topics through Instagram. Participants research various areas of medicine, craft concise and compelling content, and develop their communication skills.

Research Mentorship Program

Our Research Mentorship Program connects high school students with experienced mentors to guide them in writing research papers on topics of their choosing. Through this program, students receive personalized support in formulating research questions, conducting literature reviews, and presenting their findings on the Fellows of Meciine Newsletter
