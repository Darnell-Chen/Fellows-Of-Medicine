About Us

Fellows of Medicine is a student-led organization focused on empowering high school students with a passion for medicine and science. We connect students with mentors and industry professionals through programs like our Research Mentorship Program and Medi Intern Programt. Our goal is to provide equitable access to resources and support, allowing high schoolers to explore and pursure their passions in medicine.

Our Mission

Fellows of Medicine aims to foster equal opportunities by providing a platform for passionate high school students to network with industry professionals in the field of medicine. We are dedicated to cultivating a supportive community where experienced students in various stages of their medical journey actively guide and mentor high schoolers, facilitating a seamless transition into the dynamic world of healthcare, and ensuring that aspiring individuals have equitable access to resources, knowledge, and mentorship that will empower them to excel in their pursuit of a medical career.
